As a Qualified Learn Center we develop our staff. Our staff member are required to obtain a certain amount of training hours every year to keep them abreast to the latest learning skill for your child. We try to provide the state of the art learning tools. Our staff has required training, a set goal to obtain a Child Development Associate (CDA) certificate, there is off\on-site training, attend seminars such as: Developing Early Childhood Leaders (DECL).

What is the CDA: The Child Development Associate (CDA) Credential is the most widely recognized credential in early childhood education and is the best first step on the path of career advancement for early childhood professionals. The distinctive CDA credential is the only nationally recognized, portable, competency-based credential of its kind in the United States.
